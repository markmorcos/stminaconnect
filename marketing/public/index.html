What it does

Built for servants

Quick Add captures a new member in seconds. Check-in marks attendance for an entire group from a single roster. Follow-ups surface members who have been absent or asked to be on break, so no one is forgotten.

Built for your church

Admins see the whole community at a glance: attendance trends, follow-up coverage, servant assignments. Each volunteer's dashboard shows who they are responsible for and what is pending.

Privacy-first

Member data lives on EU infrastructure. We do not run analytics, do not run ads, and do not share data with third parties. GDPR rights — access, export, erasure — are built in from day one.
