## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)

St. Mina Connect is a pastoral-care companion for Coptic and other church communities. Servants register members, mark attendance, and follow up with people who fall out of touch — all from one focused mobile app.

Built for servants

- Quick Add captures a new member in seconds.
- Check-in marks attendance for an entire group from a single roster.
- Follow-ups surface members who have been absent or asked to be on break, so no one is forgotten.

Built for your church

- Admins see the whole community at a glance: attendance trends, follow-up coverage, servant assignments.
- The Servant Dashboard helps each volunteer see who they are responsible for and what is pending.
- Roles, assignments, and audit logs are all built in.

Privacy-first

- Member data lives on Supabase EU infrastructure.
- We do not run analytics, do not run ads, and do not share data with third parties.
- GDPR rights — access, export, erasure — are built in from day one. Servants can export or delete their account from Settings.

What's included in v1

- Registering members with name, contact info, and pastoral notes
- Quick Add for low-friction one-tap entry
- Group check-in for events, services, and meetings
- Follow-ups for absent members and on-break tracking
- Servant Dashboard with assignments and absence alerts
- Admin Compliance: data export, GDPR erasure, audit log
- English, Arabic, German throughout

Coming soon
Calendar sync, deeper reporting, and richer follow-up workflows.
